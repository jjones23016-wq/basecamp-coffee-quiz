# Coffee Personality Quiz Requirements

## Project Summary
This quiz helps Basecamp Coffee customers discover their coffee personality and get a single drink recommendation based on their answers.

## Result Type
- Single recommendation only
- Example: “You’re a Bold Adventurer! Your coffee: Double Espresso.”

## Visual Style
- Warm/cozy aesthetic
- Earth tones, soft gradients, inviting feel
- Rounded cards, gentle shadows, warm typography
- Text-first layout with small accent icons for answer options

## Images and Icons
- Images: No images for now
- Icons: Yes, small icons next to each answer option for polish

## Personality → Coffee Pairings
1. **Bold Adventurer**
   - Drink: Double Espresso
   - Tagline: “You live for intensity.”

2. **Zen Minimalist**
   - Drink: Black Coffee, Single Origin
   - Tagline: “Simple. Clean. Perfect.”

3. **Health Nut**
   - Drink: Oat Milk Americano
   - Tagline: “Wellness in every sip.”

## Quiz Logic
- Each answer maps to one personality.
- At the end, the personality with the highest score is shown.
- If there is a tie, the first personality with the top score is selected.
- Result text shows a single personality label and the recommended coffee.

## Quiz Questions
1. **What’s your perfect morning coffee moment?**
   - “A sunrise hike and something strong to kick it off.” → Bold Adventurer
   - “A slow sip while I journal or read.” → Zen Minimalist
   - “A green smoothie and a light coffee to stay balanced.” → Health Nut

2. **Pick the ideal weekend plan:**
   - “Explore a new trail or try a new brew spot.” → Bold Adventurer
   - “Do yoga, meditate, and keep it peaceful.” → Zen Minimalist
   - “Hit the farmers market and choose healthy bites.” → Health Nut

3. **Which snack sounds best right now?**
   - “Dark chocolate and a bold espresso shot.” → Bold Adventurer
   - “Simple toast with honey and a clean black coffee.” → Zen Minimalist
   - “Fresh fruit and a creamy oat milk Americano.” → Health Nut

4. **What phrase fits your vibe?**
   - “Go big or go home.” → Bold Adventurer
   - “Less fuss, more clarity.” → Zen Minimalist
   - “Nourish first, then move.” → Health Nut

5. **Your ideal coffee order is:**
   - “Double espresso with no nonsense.” → Bold Adventurer
   - “Single-origin black coffee, pure and simple.” → Zen Minimalist
   - “Oat milk Americano with a light, smooth finish.” → Health Nut

## Notes for the build
- Keep the quiz flow simple: question cards, answer buttons, result card.
- Use warm background colors and soft accent styling.
- Include small icons beside answer text for a polished feel.
- No external images are required for this first version.
- The quiz should feel friendly, approachable, and aligned with Basecamp Coffee’s brand voice.
